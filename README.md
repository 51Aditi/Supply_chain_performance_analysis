# 📦 Supply Chain Loss Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Supply Chain Loss Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence application developed using **Python, Streamlit, Plotly, Pandas, and NumPy**. The dashboard analyzes supply chain operations, sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product categories, and delivery efficiency to identify operational bottlenecks and provide actionable business recommendations.

The project enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), understand business performance across markets, and make data-driven decisions to improve profitability and customer satisfaction.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Evaluate delivery efficiency and shipping delays.
* Identify high-performing and underperforming markets.
* Understand customer segment profitability.
* Discover top-performing product categories.
* Detect operational inefficiencies causing revenue leakage.
* Generate strategic recommendations for management.

## 📌 Key Business Insights

### Revenue Performance

* Total Sales exceeded $36 Million.
* Europe and LATAM contribute the highest revenue.

### Profitability

* Overall profit margin is approximately 10.78%.
* Profit generation varies significantly across markets.

### Delivery Performance

* Late deliveries represent a major operational challenge.
* Delivery delays negatively impact customer experience and profitability.

### Customer Segments

* Consumer segment drives the majority of sales.
* Corporate customers generate stronger profit per order.

### Product Categories

* Fishing, Cleats, and Camping & Hiking are among the top-performing categories.
* Profit is concentrated in a limited number of product categories.
